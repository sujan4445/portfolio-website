Honors & Awards

Golden Jubilee Scholarship Scheme (GJSS)

Issued by Embassy of India · Jul 2022

The Embassy of India, Kathmandu annually awards scholarships to 200 meritorious Nepalese students to pursue undergraduate studies at institutions across Nepal. This competitive program promotes academic excellence and strengthens India-Nepal educational ties by supporting high-achieving students from diverse backgrounds. By easing financial constraints, the scholarship enhances access to quality higher education and empowers youth to realize their academic potential.

Mahatma Gandhi Scholarship Scheme (MGSS)

Issued by Embassy of India · Jul 2020

The Embassy of India awards Mahatma Gandhi scholarship to 2000 students annually from Nepal. This scholarship is mainly provided to outstanding students with good performance at SLC/SEE examinations to pursue higher education.
